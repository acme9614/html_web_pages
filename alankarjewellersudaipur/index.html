It all began in 1951...

In the narrow lanes of Udaipur, where tradition whispers through every stone and the scent of sandalwood lingers in the air, a young man named Shri Ramchandra Sarraf started something quietly beautiful. With hands skilled in heritage craftsmanship and a heart full of devotion, he began creating jewelry, not for showrooms, but for people. For brides, for families, for stories. Each piece he made carried not just gold, but emotion.

1980 – A Dream Finds a Name

The work spoke for itself. Word spread, and love for his craft grew. That’s when Alankar Jewellers was born. With a simple promise to offer jewelry that felt personal, pure, and timeless. Not mass-produced, but handcrafted. Not flashy, but full of soul. Slowly, families began to trust us with their milestones. Engagements. Weddings. Anniversaries. It wasn’t just a store anymore. It was becoming part of their legacy.

1995 – A Son Steps In, A Legacy Grows

As the years passed, Mr Mohan Makhija & Mr Suresh Makhija stepped in to carry the torch. He didn’t just inherit a business, he inherited a purpose. To hold onto everything that made Alankar special, while building something even more meaningful. Under his gentle guidance, the store expanded, the designs grew richer, and the love from the people only deepened.
