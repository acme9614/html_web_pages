OUR STORY

Kevabox is a subsidiary of Civacart Retail PVT LTD. Kevabox is a modernistic initiative by City Gold and Diamonds, with a legacy spanning over 25 years of unbridled customer satisfaction across multiple store locations in Kerala and Karnataka.

We absolutely felt it was the right time to present our delectable collection of ornaments crafted out of gold, silver, platinum with diamonds and other gems to you, using cutting edge technology, for your purchasing ease. Our journey at City Gold and Diamonds has brought us to this point in time as the people’s favourite brand of jewellery when it comes to shopping for sparkling finery, immersing in an inclusive eShop where we can evolve our business practices to this next level for a new generation with all this latest technology available at their fingertips.

We will continue to place our customer’s thoughts and demands as our cornerstone of all merchandising policy on Kevabox, the same way we uphold these values at our physical store locations of City Gold and Diamonds.
